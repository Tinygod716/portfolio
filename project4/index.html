Project04 - Aggie Square

Aggie Square
Environmental Design

This is a collaborative environmental design project developed around Aggie Square, UC Davis’s innovation district in Sacramento. Centered on the relationship between community, public space, and cross-sector collaboration, the project explores how spatial design can support more inclusive community services and stronger connections between the university, local residents, and surrounding urban resources.

Role Graphic Design & Landscape Sketching
