AR Glasses

A big part of the inspiration for this project came from my father. As I watched his daily routines, I started noticing the small inconveniences and frustrations that came with changes in his body and habits. They were not dramatic problems, but they appeared again and again in everyday life. Seeing this made me think about how design could offer support in a gentler and more practical way.
